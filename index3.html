Eat Happy. Be Happy.

Pie on Life is a consignment bakery based out of Kingston, NY. We specialize in seasonal pies that showcase the bounty of the Hudson Valley.

Think strawberry rhubarb and sour cherry hand pies, apple oatmeal crumbles, savory vegetable quiches, and more. Also on offer are jams and pie fillings, perfect for when you want to make something fresh at home but are feeling pressed for time.

*We have vegan pies and frozen vegan pie dough available by request!*

*We can be found most Saturday's at the Mower's Flea Market in Woodstock.*

*We do take custom orders and are available to cater your next special event with advance notice.*
